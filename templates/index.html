About this Project

The AI CyberShield Matrix is a next-generation cybersecurity toolkit designed to protect individuals and small businesses from phishing websites, malware attacks, and other digital threats. Using the power of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, this project analyzes suspicious links, files, and activities in real-time, ensuring users can safely browse, work, and communicate online.



This project has been developed as part of an academic initiative at K. K. Wagh Polytechnic, Nashik, under the Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML) Department. The goal is to bridge the gap between academic research and practical industry applications by building an intelligent, easy-to-use security solution for the modern digital world.



The system integrates advanced AI models, threat-detection algorithms, and a simple user interface to make cybersecurity accessible even to non-technical users. It demonstrates how AIML technologies can be applied to real-world problems and showcases the innovative spirit of the students and faculty of K. K. Wagh Polytechnic.



This project not only reflects our technical learning but also our commitment to creating solutions that contribute to a safer digital ecosystem.



This project is proudly sponsored and mentored by Cyber Sanskar, Nashik, an organization dedicated to fostering a secure digital culture and providing expert cybersecurity education. Founded by Mr. Tanmay S. Dikshit Sir, Cyber Sanskar has played a pivotal role in guiding this initiative. Through their technical expertise and vision for a "Cyber-Safe India," we have been able to integrate industry-standard threat analysis techniques and ethical security practices into the AI CyberShield Matrix.
